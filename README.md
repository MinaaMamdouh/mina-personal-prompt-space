# Prompt Engineering | بالعربي

Welcome to **Prompt Engineering | بالعربي** — the first Arabic initiative dedicated to simplifying **Prompt Engineering** in a clear and easy way.  
All content is **free**, **structured**, and **100% in Arabic**.

---

## Our Mission

I'm **Mina Mamdouh**, content creator and prompt engineer behind this project.  
Our goal is to make prompt engineering accessible to everyone interested in AI tools, and help apply it practically in daily life.

---

## What You'll Find Here

- Simple explanations of prompt engineering basics.  
- Ready-to-use prompts for AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and more.  
- Real-world use cases in writing, coding, education, business, and beyond.  
- An organized Notion workspace (coming soon) — *my personal space to share exclusive prompts*.  
- Interactive challenges and community contributions to enrich the knowledge base.
